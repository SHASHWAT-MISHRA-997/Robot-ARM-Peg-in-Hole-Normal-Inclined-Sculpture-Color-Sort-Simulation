Dual Workbench Guide
The double robot page now follows the same dashboard structure as the main simulation, but with mirrored control and telemetry for both cells.
Use the left panel to assign surface and color sort tasks per robot, keep the center stage focused on the selected setup only, and use the right panel for live dual telemetry, grasp quality, force, slip, and pose details.
